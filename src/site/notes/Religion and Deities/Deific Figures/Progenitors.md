## List of Progenitor Demons

[[Religion and Deities/Deific Figures/Projenitors/Azure, the Blue Progenitor Demon\|Azure, the Blue Progenitor Demon]] - Master of manipulation and betrayal, she teaches that personal advancement justifies any deception and that allies exist only until better opportunities emerge.

[[Religion and Deities/Deific Figures/Projenitors/Blanc, the White Progenitor Demon\|Blanc, the White Progenitor Demon]] - Embodiment of life's emptiness, she embraces the void and teaches isolation as the only honest response to existence's inherent meaninglessness.

[[Religion and Deities/Deific Figures/Projenitors/Jaune, the Yellow Progenitor Demon\|Jaune, the Yellow Progenitor Demon]] - Breaker of minds, she wields fear and madness as weapons, shattering sanity to achieve personal power over those who would oppose her.

[[Religion and Deities/Deific Figures/Projenitors/Noir, the Black Progenitor Demon\|Noir, the Black Progenitor Demon]] - Lord of hierarchy, he teaches that strength entitles dominance, that power flows to those who seize it, and that natural order demands the strong rule the weak.

[[Religion and Deities/Deific Figures/Projenitors/Rouge, the Red Progenitor Demon\|Rouge, the Red Progenitor Demon]] - Champion of absolute freedom, he embodies passion and fighting for what you believe in, breaking all chains that would constrain your individual will.

[[Religion and Deities/Deific Figures/Projenitors/Vert, the Green Progenitor Demon\|Vert, the Green Progenitor Demon]] - Lady of envy, she declares that all things belong to whoever desires them most, and that taking what you want is simply claiming your rightful due.

[[Religion and Deities/Deific Figures/Projenitors/Viole, the Purple Progenitor Demon\|Viole, the Purple Progenitor Demon]] - Master of nobility, he embodies the divine right to rule and the belief that the world exists for those bold enough to reach out and take it.

## List of Progenitor Angels

[[Religion and Deities/Deific Figures/Projenitors/Kollane, the Yellow Progenitor Angel\|Kollane, the Yellow Progenitor Angel]] - Banisher of sorrow, he enforces eternal joy and courage, teaching that fear and sadness are selfish indulgences weakening the collective.

[[Religion and Deities/Deific Figures/Projenitors/Lilla, the Purple Progenitor Angel\|Lilla, the Purple Progenitor Angel]] - Voice of the many, she embodies perfect harmony through collective consciousness, teaching that individual thought disrupts the unity all must achieve.

[[Religion and Deities/Deific Figures/Projenitors/Must, the Black Progenitor Angel\|Must, the Black Progenitor Angel]] - Servant of order, he teaches that surrendering individual will to rightful authority serves the collective good better than any personal ambition.

[[Religion and Deities/Deific Figures/Projenitors/Punane, the Red Progenitor Angel\|Punane, the Red Progenitor Angel]] - The eternal martyr, he embodies passion directed entirely toward others' causes, teaching that individual survival is selfishness when others need champions.

[[Religion and Deities/Deific Figures/Projenitors/Roheline, the Green Progenitor Angel\|Roheline, the Green Progenitor Angel]] - The infinite giver, she teaches that all possessions are theft from the collective and that true virtue demands giving until nothing remains.

[[Religion and Deities/Deific Figures/Projenitors/Sinine, the Blue Progenitor Angel\|Sinine, the Blue Progenitor Angel]] - Speaker of all truths, she demands radical transparency in all things, believing secrets are selfish acts that divide communities.

[[Religion and Deities/Deific Figures/Projenitors/Valge, the White Progenitor Angel\|Valge, the White Progenitor Angel]] - Validator of souls, he teaches that empty lives gain meaning only through complete selfless service to the collective good.
