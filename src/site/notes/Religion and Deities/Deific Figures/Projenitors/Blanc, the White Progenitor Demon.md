# Blanc, White Progenitor Demon
*"Void of the Soul" â€¢ "Lady of Distance"*

## Overview

Blanc (blahnk), Void of the Soul, rules over a realm of [[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Gray Waste of Hades\|Hades]] known as the Blank Expanse. She is the embodiment of the emptiness of life, and can be felt by all who experience the call of the void.
## Worship and Clergy

### Domains
**Granted Domains:** Death, Repose, Travel, Void

**Subdomains:** Isolation, Murder, Portals, Souls

### Holy Symbol
A doorway opening to nothing

### Sacred Elements

**Sacred Animal:** Owl

**Sacred Colors:** White and Grey

### Worshipers

Travelers and self isolationists form Blanc's following.

### Tenets of Faith

1. **My soul is empty, for nothing in existence is truly filled.**

2. **That which lives is destined to die, and should not fear such inevitable events.**

3. **Life is truly without meaning, so I must give this meaningless life of mine some use.**

## Areas of Concern

Emptiness, Distance, Death

## Adjective

Blancian (BLAHN-see-an)
