# Vert, Green Progenitor Demon

## Overview

Vert (vairt), Lady of Envy, rules over the [[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Tarterian Depths of Carceri\|Carcerian]] realm known as the Pilfering Plains. She embodies the idea of taking what you want when you want.

## Worship and Clergy

### Domains
**Granted Domains:** Animal, Knowledge, Luck, Trickery

**Subdomains:** Curse, Insect, Memory, Thievery

### Holy Symbol
An outstretched hand clutching overflowing coins in green and brown
### Sacred Elements

**Sacred Animal:** Cat

**Sacred Colors:** Green and Brown

### Worshipers
Businessmen and dragons form Vert's following.

### Tenets of Faith

1. **That which belongs to me is mine. That which belongs to others is also mine.**

2. **I am not lucky. I simply make my own luck from others.**

3. **I am not simply taking others' belongings I want. I am unburdening the weak by giving the burden to myself.**

## Areas of Concern

Luck, Envy, Greed

## Adjective

Vertian (VAIR-tee-an)
