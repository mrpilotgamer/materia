# Must, Black Progenitor Angel
*"Servant of Order" â€¢ "The Perfect Instrument"*

## Overview

Must (MOOST), Servant of Order, resides over a section of [[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Seven Mounting Heavens of Celestia\|Celestia]] known as the Hall of Eternal Vigil. He embodies the idea of perfect obedience to rightful authority, teaching that true freedom comes from surrendering individual will to those wiser and more powerful, and that the highest calling is to become a flawless instrument of benevolent hierarchies.

## Worship and Clergy

### Domains
**Granted Domains:** Community, Glory, Good, Law

**Subdomains:** Archon, Cooperation, Honor, Loyalty

### Holy Symbol
A crown above an open palm in black and gold
### Sacred Elements

**Sacred Animal:** Dog

**Sacred Colors:** Black and Gold

### Worshipers
Devoted soldiers and servants form Must's following.

### Tenets of Faith

1. **Obedience to rightful authority is the highest virtue.** I shall surrender my will to those placed above me, for they see what I cannot.

2. **The hierarchy exists for the collective good.** I shall trust that my place serves a greater purpose, even when I cannot comprehend it.

3. **Individual desires disrupt perfect order.** I shall become an instrument, setting aside personal ambition to serve the structure that protects all.

## Areas of Concern

Obedience, Hierarchy, Service

## Adjective

Mustian (MOOS-tee-an)
