*"Leader of the Strong" • "Manipulator of Law"*

## Overview

Noir (no-AHR), Leader of the Strong, rules over the [[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Nine Hells of Baator\|Baatorian]] realm known as the Darkened Badlands. He embodies the idea of strength and hierarchies, and is known for having limited control over some laws of nature.

## Worship and Clergy

### Domains
**Granted Domains:** Destruction, Magic, Rune, Strength

**Subdomains:** Arcane, Catastrophe, Ferocity, Legislation

### Holy Symbol
A stepped pyramid in black and red

### Sacred Elements

**Sacred Animal:** Eagle

**Sacred Colors:** Black and Red

### Worshipers
Dictators and warriors form Noir's following.

### Tenets of Faith

1. **The strong rule over the weak. The weak follow the strong.**

2. **Power is ever-changing. Ensure it changes in your favor.**

3. **I must know my place in the hierarchy and follow it appropriately.**

## Areas of Concern

Power, Hierarchies, Dominance

## Adjective

Noirian (no-AIR-ee-an)
