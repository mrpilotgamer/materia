# Viole, Purple Progenitor Demon

## Overview

Viole (vee-OH-ley), Lord of Nobility, rules over the [[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Infernal Battlefield of Acheron\|Acheronean]] realm known as the Amethyst Kingdom. He is the embodiment of the lavish life, and the right to rule.

## Worship and Clergy

### Domains
**Granted Domains:** Charm, Glory, Nobility, Trickery

**Subdomains:** Aristocracy, Captivation, Espionage, Hubris

### Holy Symbol
A golden crown pierced by a coiled purple serpent

### Sacred Elements

**Sacred Animal:** Snake

**Sacred Colors:** Purple and Gold

### Worshipers
Nobles and monarchs form Viole's following.

### Tenets of Faith

1. **All the world is mine, I must simply reach out and take it.**

2. **Those who are below me must be reminded of such.**

3. **Others' minds are mine to manipulate, so long as it betters my position in life.**

## Areas of Concern

Nobility, Armies, Political Schemes

## Adjective

Violian (vee-OH-lee-an)
