# Valge, White Progenitor Angel

## Overview

Valge (VAHL-geh), Validator of Souls, resides over a section of [[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Blessed Fields of Elysium\|Elysium]] known as the Gathering Glade. He represents the filling of the void, and allowing empty lives to gain meaning through selfless works.

## Worship and Clergy

### Domains
**Granted Domains:** Death, Glory, Liberation, Repose

**Subdomains:** Honor, Psychopomp, Self-Realization, Souls

### Holy Symbol
A lark rising from cupped hands in white and grey

### Sacred Elements

**Sacred Animal:** Lark

**Sacred Colors:** White and Grey

### Worshipers
Travellers and Death-or-Glory Warriors form Valge's following.

### Tenets of Faith

1. **My soul is empty, and it must be filled with my lord's light.**

2. **Our lives will one day end, so we must spend our days filling that life with meaning for others.**

3. **When we die, we do so knowing it matters not, for our lives serve the collective, and bring greater good for all.**

## Areas of Concern

Fullness, Closeness, Death

## Adjective

Valgean (VAHL-gee-an)
