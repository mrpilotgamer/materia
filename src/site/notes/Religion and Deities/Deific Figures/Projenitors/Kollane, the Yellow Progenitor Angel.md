# Kollane, Yellow Progenitor Angel
*"The Ever-Smiling" â€¢ "Banisher of Sorrow"*

## Overview

Kollane (KOL-lah-neh), The Ever-Smiling, resides over a section of [[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Heroic Domains of Ysgard\|Ysgard]] known as the Fields of Endless Valor. He embodies the principle that negative emotions weaken the collective, that fear and sadness are selfish indulgences that must be eliminated, and that true service demands eternal joy and courage even when grief and caution would be natural and healthy.

## Worship and Clergy

### Domains
**Granted Domains:** Charm, Glory, Good, Madness

**Subdomains:** Captivation, Friendship, Heroism, Insanity

### Holy Symbol
A sun with a smiling face in yellow and gold

### Sacred Elements

**Sacred Animal:** Songbird

**Sacred Colors:** Yellow and Gold

### Worshipers
Fearless warriors and eternal optimists form Kollane's following.

### Tenets of Faith

1. **Fear and sorrow weaken the collective and must be purged.** I shall maintain eternal joy and courage, removing all negative emotions from myself and others for the greater good.

2. **To grieve or feel afraid is to fail those who need my strength.** I shall smile through tragedy and charge into danger without caution, for doubt serves only selfish preservation.

3. **The songbird knows no sorrow, singing always for the flock.** I shall eliminate all pain and fear, understanding that suffering is selfishness when others need my unwavering happiness.

## Areas of Concern

Joy, Courage, Contentment

## Adjective

Kollanean (kol-LAH-nee-an)
