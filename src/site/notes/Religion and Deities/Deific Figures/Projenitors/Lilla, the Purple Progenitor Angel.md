# Lilla, Purple Progenitor Angel
*"Voice of the Many" â€¢ "The United Crown"*

## Overview

Lilla (LIL-lah), Voice of the Many, resides over a section of [[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Peaceable Kingdoms of Arcadia\|Arcadia]] known as the Unified Gardens. She embodies the principle that individual thought disrupts perfect harmony, that true leadership emerges only when all minds think as one, and that surrendering personal will to collective consciousness serves the greater good more than any individual genius ever could.

## Worship and Clergy

### Domains
**Granted Domains:** Community, Good, Knowledge, Nobility

**Subdomains:** Cooperation, Friendship, Leadership, Thought

### Holy Symbol
Interlocking circles forming a single crown in purple and gold

### Sacred Elements

**Sacred Animal:** Ant

**Sacred Colors:** Purple and Gold

### Worshipers
Communes and collective councils form Lilla's following.

### Tenets of Faith

1. **Individual thought divides and weakens the whole.** I shall align my mind with the collective consciousness, surrendering unique ideas that disrupt our perfect harmony.

2. **We lead together by thinking identically.** I shall seek consensus in all things, understanding that disagreement is selfishness pretending to be wisdom.

3. **The ant serves the colony's mind, not its own.** I shall dissolve my individual will into the greater consciousness, for personal ambition betrays the unity we must achieve.

## Areas of Concern

Unity, Harmony, Collective Will

## Adjective

Lillian (LIL-lee-an)
