# Sinine, Blue Progenitor Angel
*"Speaker of All Truths" â€¢ "The Unveiled One"*

## Overview

Sinine (SIH-nih-neh), Speaker of All Truths, resides over a section of [[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Twin Paradises of Bytopia\|Bytopia]] known as the Hall of Unveiled Words. She embodies the principle that all truth must be shared for the collective good, that secrets are inherently selfish acts that divide communities, and that radical transparency in all things serves the greater whole even when individual feelings suffer.

## Worship and Clergy

### Domains
**Granted Domains:** Community, Good, Knowledge, Law

**Subdomains:** Cooperation, Friendship, Thought, Truth

### Holy Symbol
An open book with no cover in blue and silver

### Sacred Elements

**Sacred Animal:** Mirror Beetle

**Sacred Colors:** Blue and Silver

### Worshipers
Judges and confessors form Sinine's following.

### Tenets of Faith

1. **All truths must be spoken, regardless of consequence.** I shall share every truth I know, for withholding knowledge serves only selfish comfort.

2. **Secrets are acts of division and selfishness.** I shall expose all hidden things, understanding that privacy weakens the bonds of community.

3. **Honesty serves the collective even when it wounds the individual.** I shall speak brutal truths for the greater good, knowing that feelings matter less than transparency.

## Areas of Concern

Truth, Transparency, Honesty

## Adjective

Sininean (sih-NIH-nee-an)
