# Common races

[[Species and Cultures/Races/Araki\|Araki]] - Tall, strong, and varied in skill sets, the Araki are capable of many great deeds. Though their outward appearance looks fierce and muscular, they are much more skilled in skills requiring finesse. Most notably, Araki are also known for how difficult it is for them to die.

[[Species and Cultures/Races/Chisana\|Chisana]] - Quick and nimble, these fey creatures live across the world. Most chisana pay respect and worship one of the primordial spirits, because of their fey ancestry. Though they lack a lot of strength, they make up for it with their quick reflexes, and quick wit.

[[Species and Cultures/Races/Daemedi\|Daemedi]] - Descended from denizens of the lower planes, Daemedi is a term used to describe those who have infernal, abyssal, or any similar bloodline within them. Their appearance ranges from nearly human, to a humanoid devil or demon, and anywhere in between. They are most known fir there detection abilities, and theyre ability to change form to another humanoid

[[Species and Cultures/Races/Drakmed\|Drakmed]] - Descended from true-dragons, Drakmed are hardy creatures, capable of taking many blows before falling in battle. They are also known for their small Draconis Fundamentum, the gland dragons use to spew their dragon breath

[[Species and Cultures/Races/Grendal\|Grendal]] - Small and fleet of foot, Grendals have an instinct to keep away from combat whenever possible. However, in combat, they are known for their uncanny self-preservation instincts. They are notably amongst the most skilled in diplomacy, and the art of deception.

[[Species and Cultures/Races/Human\|Human]] - Curious, cunning, and enduring, humans have survived through sheer stubbornness, and clever solutions to complex problems. Despite their lack of scales, or thick skin, they appear to be on par with the drakmed for their ability to stay alive.

[[Species and Cultures/Races/Mannee\|Mannee]] - Mannee are small, meek, avian-like humanoids. They are covered in feathers from head-to-toe, but beyond that look similar to humans. They are known for being very well practitioner magic casters. They are also Unisex, with the ability to change their appearance at will, to most of the humanoid races, with but a thought.

[[Species and Cultures/Races/Trox\|Trox]] - Trox are powerfully built insect-like humanoids. Originating in subterranean caverns, they walk on two strong legs, have two “arms” used for wielding weapons and performing heavy labor, and four smaller arms extending from their midsection, which are mainly vestigial, save small simple tasks, such as holding small objects.

[[Species and Cultures/Races/Vullukk\|Vullukk]] - Curious, and climbers by nature, the Vullukk are a very intelligent people, along side their nimble characteristics. Many vullukk naturally pick up the long tradition of collecting tidbits of trivia from wherever they can, learning about the world one factoid at a time.

[[Species and Cultures/Races/Yol-Mar\|Yol-Mar]] - Descended from ancient fire giants, the Yol-Mar are a people known for being strong, capable, and passionate. Their blood innately gives them some of the abilities their ancestors had, as their weapons turn red hot, and their skin is resistant to burns.
