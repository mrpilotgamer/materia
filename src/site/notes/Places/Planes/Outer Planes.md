# Outer Planes

[[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Peaceable Kingdoms of Arcadia\|Peaceable Kingdoms of Arcadia]] - Two layers of geometric perfection where einheriar enforce harmony so rigidly that the third layer already transmigrated to Mechanus.

[[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Seven Mounting Heavens of Celestia\|Seven Mounting Heavens of Celestia]] - A sacred mountain ascending through seven layers of trials, where archons guide souls toward ultimate union with law and good.

[[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Twin Paradises of Bytopia\|Twin Paradises of Bytopia]] - Two layers facing each other across a mile of open space with reversing gravity, contrasting pastoral Dothion with untamed Shurrock.

[[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Blessed Fields of Elysium\|Blessed Fields of Elysium]] - Four layers of ultimate goodness where the River Oceanus flows through pastoral landscapes of peace, kindness, and simple joy.

[[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Wilderness of the Beastlands\|Wilderness of the Beastlands]] - Three layers frozen at dawn, dusk, and night where celestial animals hunt, and the spirits of loyal companions find paradise.

[[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Olympian Glades of Arborea\|Olympian Glades of Arborea]] - - Three layers of passion and beauty where mannee lords and azatas revel amid mercurial weather and overwhelming natural splendor.

[[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Heroic Domains of Ysgard\|Heroic Domains of Ysgard]] - Three layers of floating earthbergs where warriors battle eternally, healing rapidly to fight again beneath the Bifrost's glow.

[[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Ever-Changing Chaos of Limbo\|Ever-Changing Chaos of Limbo]] - Raw elemental soup where reality bends to willpower, home to githzerai monasteries and the slaadi Spawning Stone.

[[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Windswept Depths of Pandemonium\|Windswept Depths of Pandemonium]] - Four layers of howling tunnels carved through solid matter, where screaming winds steal sound, heat, and sanity itself.

[[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Infinite Layers of the Abyss\|Infinite Layers of the Abyss]] - Chaotic evil incarnate across 666+ layers, each ruled by demon lords in their twisted domains of suffering.

[[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Tarterian Depths of Carceri\|Tarterian Depths of Carceri]] - Six nested layers of orblike prison-worlds where traitors and exiles plot revenge amid constant betrayal and crushing despair.

[[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Gray Waste of Hades\|Gray Waste of Hades]] - Three glooms of spiritual decay and the Blood War's central battlefield, where hope withers and apathy consumes all.

[[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Bleak Eternity of Gehenna\|Bleak Eternity of Gehenna]] - Four volcanic layers of treacherous 45-degree slopes where yugoloths scheme and gravity pulls travelers toward fiery doom.

[[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Nine Hells of Baator\|Nine Hells of Baator]] - Nine descending layers of lawful tyranny where Asmodeus and his archdevils rule through contracts, suffering, and infernal hierarchy.

[[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Infernal Battlefield of Acheron\|Infernal Battlefield of Acheron]] - Four layers of iron cubes crashing through the void, where endless armies wage war for glory, tyranny, and martial dominance.

[[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Clockwork Nirvana of Mechanus\|Clockwork Nirvana of Mechanus]] - Infinite interlocking cogs turning in perfect mathematical harmony, where inevitables enforce absolute law without mercy or malice.

[[Places/Planes/Outer Planes/Concordant Domains of the Outlands\|Concordant Domains of the Outlands]] - The neutral hub of the multiverse where magic weakens with distance from the central Spire, topped by Sigil, the City of Doors.
